# Notes

## Website goals and purpose

- The website is a portfolio of my work and my skills
- The website is a platform for me to share my work and my skills
- The website is a platform for me to share my thoughts and insights
- The target audience is job seekers, recruiters, and other professionals in the field of web development and software engineering coming to this website to check my skills and work
- I am not selling anything on this website, it is just proof of my work and my skills (No real sale pitch and no real sales funnel)

## Website structure

- Home page (Dashboard page with overview of my work and my skills)
- Work page (My previous works page with list of my previous works)
- Projects page (My side projects and experiments page with list of my side projects and experiments)
- Insights page (My thoughts and insights page with list of my thoughts and insights)
- About page (About me page with about me information)
- Contact page (Contact me page with contact me information)
